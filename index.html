Two-Factor Identification

In this project, I developed a wrist-worn system with a heart rate sensor and accelerometer. I used a neural network to verify identity using heart rate and gait characteristics. I achieved 95% identification accuracy.

Autonomous Underwater Robot

In this project, I designed and built an autonomous robot to collect data in a pool and in a lake. I incorporated thermocouples, turbidity sensors, pressure sensors, gyroscopes, and accelerometers. Measured temperature, turbidity, and depth. The tools I used included COMSOL (fluid dynamics), Arduino, LabVIEW, and oscilloscopes.

I repaired and restored the vehicle to perfect operating condition, having driven 10,000 miles since restoration without any critical failures.
